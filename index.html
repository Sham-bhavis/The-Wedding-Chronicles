Our Story

~ A journey from hello to forever

Chapter 1: Where It All Began

In 2018, during their first year at Amity University, Astha and Janmejay met through mutual friends.
What started as a simple introduction soon became the beginning of something truly special.

"Every forever begins with a simple hello"

Chapter 2: Friendship & Love

From food adventures and college memories to helping each other through academics,
they found joy in every little moment. Before they knew it, friendship had blossomed into love.

"The best love stories begin with friendship"

Chapter 3: Growing Together

After graduation, they explored places like Goa and Mussoorie,
built successful careers, and continued dreaming side by side.

"Every journey is better when travelled together"

Chapter 4: The Promise Forever

In 2025, they celebrated a beautiful ring ceremony, followed by a dreamy proposal in December.
And on 21 November 2026, they begin their most beautiful chapter yet—forever.

"And so, they chose forever"
